## Shipped Products

### Opus Loop
**Meditation & Focus Timer** — $4.99, iOS
The flagship. Timer with background sounds for meditation, deep work, and physical activity. One-time purchase, no subscription, custom mp3 uploads, Apple Music integration. See `/15_Documentation/Opus_Loop/` for full documentation.

### Fjorden Camera App
**iOS Camera App** — for Fjorden (Norwegian camera accessory company)
Professional camera app with rangefinder-style UI, photo/video modes, MagSafe adapter support, full auto and pro modes. Extensive feature set including widgets, tutorials, social media management tools. See `12_Fjorden_App/` for detailed notes.

### Ante
**iOS App** — for Ante.org
Product design and development including onboarding, BTC pathway features, wallet integration, design system (V1), metrics tracking, and QA. See `11_Ante_App/` for detailed notes.

## In Development

### CyniCal
**Friendship Maintenance App** — iOS
"A cynical and pragmatic way of trying to be a very good friend." Tracks friends, gifts, dates, check-ins. V1 built in SwiftUI/SwiftData. V2 vision includes friend scores, data gifts, sarcastic notifications, and ASSN integration. See `/15_Documentation/ASSN_CyniCal_1stDegree/` for full documentation.

Target: Launch alongside Opus Loop with iOS 26.

## The Product Ecosystem Vision

OPUS products share a philosophy but serve different needs:

| Product | Need | Status |
|---------|------|--------|
| Opus Loop | Meditation & focus | Shipped |
| CyniCal | Friendship maintenance | V1 built, V2 planned |
| OPUS List | Simple task/note management | Conceptual |
| miniTask | Minimal task tracking | Conceptual |
| Score | Tracking/scoring | Conceptual |
| Sisypuss | TBD | Conceptual |
| OPUS Clubhouse | Office/workspace management | Conceptual |

The common thread: each product does one thing well, charges once (or is free), respects privacy, and has more personality than the category leader.
